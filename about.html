elementuse hosts a concise list of raw materials and their uses.

Elementuse will have graphs for every element on the periodic table and every material listed in, the United States Geological Surveys, annual, mineral commodity summaries.

In the near future we will began publishing “elementuse notation” expressions for basic processes that convert raw materials into products, that are themselves, raw materials.

elementuse example:

galvanize{(zinc)(steel)} = galvanized steel

Every man made object is created with a process and a material. Study the raw materials of earth, and the processes of humanity. When inspiration strikes, you’ll have the tools to build.
