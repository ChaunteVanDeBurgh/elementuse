Why are there materials on this site that aren't chemical elements?

Element use displays information primarily, but not exclusively, about commodities, materials, and very common products, commonly used for constructing other products. The information here is not limited to chemical elements.

Where does the DATA displayed here come from?

All of the statistics on this site were published by The United States Geological Survey, in their annual mineral commodity summaries.

Who is this data for?

Anyone! The data is displayed in this manor to give an industry outsider, insight into how typical engineering processes work, and what materials they use.
